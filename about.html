About Us

Welcome to Rifehn

Rural Initiative for Enhanced Health Network (Rifehn) is a community-based organization dedicated to improving the lives of people in rural Uganda. We believe that every community, no matter how remote, deserves access to quality health services, disaster response, education, and sustainable solutions for everyday challenges.

Since our founding, Rifehn has worked alongside local leaders, volunteers, and partners to address critical needs including healthcare outreach, disaster preparedness, education support, and livelihood empowerment. Our mission is to create lasting impact by strengthening communities with the knowledge, tools, and resources they need to thrive.

Together, we aim to build healthier, safer, and more resilient communities across Uganda.
